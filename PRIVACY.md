# Privacy Policy for TimeCurrency

**Last Updated:** December 14, 2025

TimeCurrency ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our browser extension processes information.

### 1. Data Collection
TimeCurrency does **not** collect, transmit, or sell any user data. 
- **Hourly Wage:** Your hourly wage input is stored locally on your device using Chrome's `storage.sync` API. It is never sent to any external server.
- **Browsing Activity:** The extension scans web pages solely to identify prices and convert them locally. No browsing history or page content is transmitted to us.

### 2. Third-Party Services
We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking tools.

### 3. Contact
If you have questions about this policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository.
